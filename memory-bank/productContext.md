# Product Context: Tesera

## Why this project exists
SMEs, cooperatives, and boutique e-commerce businesses often struggle with managing multiple operational channels. Handling customer communication, shipment tracking, inventory management, and operational workflows manually leads to inefficiencies, errors, and high operational costs. Tesera exists to solve this by providing an AI-first, centralized platform.

## Problems it solves
- Fragmented customer communication across multiple channels (WhatsApp, email, chat).
- Manual and error-prone inventory tracking leading to stockouts or overstock.
- Lack of visibility into shipment delays and cargo tracking.
- Disorganized team workflows and manual task assignment.
- Lack of actionable analytics and forecasting for small businesses.

## How it should work
Tesera works as an intelligent operations assistant. Upon onboarding, businesses set up their company profile, sales channels, shipment providers, and inventory preferences. The platform uses AI agents to automate responses, monitor stock levels, track shipments, and generate tasks. The user interacts primarily with a clean, uncrowded dashboard that provides a clear overview and deep-dives when necessary.

## User Experience Goals
- **Premium & Modern:** The product must feel top-tier, similar to Linear, Stripe, Notion, Vercel, Retool, or Slack admin panels.
- **Calm & Intelligent:** Avoid cluttered interfaces. The dashboard must never feel crowded.
- **Minimal Cognitive Load:** Provide strong hierarchy, clear typography, consistent spacing, and card-based layouts.
- **Guided Setup:** Include a friendly mascot during the onboarding flow to make the setup feel conversational and approachable.
- **Responsive & Accessible:** Ensure a flawless experience across mobile and desktop.
